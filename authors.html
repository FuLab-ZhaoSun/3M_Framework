Research Team

This research was primarily conducted by the Gastrointestinal Surgery team at the First Affiliated Hospital of Zhengzhou University. The team possesses extensive clinical experience and research background, focusing on oncology and multi-omics research, dedicated to promoting the standardization of biobanks and the development of precision medicine.

Research Institution

The First Affiliated Hospital of Zhengzhou University is a major medical teaching and research institution in China, equipped with advanced multi-omics research platforms and comprehensive biobank infrastructure. The hospital is committed to translational medicine research, facilitating the translation of basic research findings into clinical applications.
